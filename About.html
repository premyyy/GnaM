About The Society:

Mahatma Basaveshwar Education Society, Latur was established in 1963 with the aim of beginning the intellectual awakening & transformation in Educational, Economical, Social and cultural field of Maharashtra, specially the backward area of the Marathwada. In pursuance of the main objective society had started the College of Engineering in year 1993, at Ambajogai, to uplift rural student Community of Maharashtra, and to bring them into the main stream of Science and Technology, keeping in pace with the widening horizons of Engineering education, by offering advanced courses. In pursuance of this objective our society opened and educational Institute which is approved by both A.I.C.T.E., New Delhi and Govt. of Maharashtra namely College of Engineering in 1993.

About The Institution:

Mahatma Basaveshwar Education Society's College of Engineering Ambajogai was Established in 1993, Approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi & Affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere. It has a mission to develop a new generation of techno wizards who aspire to be the elite of the ever-growing Information Technology world. The college has built an ambiance where continuous learning, creativity and innovation are a way of life. The college supported by a strong group of well-qualified and competent faculty has a wide exposure of industry and academia.
