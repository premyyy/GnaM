COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING, AMBAJOGAI

Vision:

To facilitate transformation of students with technical knowledge to meet the changing needs of industry and society with human values.

Core Values of the Institute:

Student and employee work together to build, maintain learning and mentoring environment where:

• Vision and mission of the institute is practiced

• Professional ethics is followed

• Leadership qualities are inculcated

• Unbiased education is encouraged

• Needs of the stakeholders are focused

Mission:

* To offer state-of-the-art facilities for the students of all programs.

* To pursue academic excellence through student centric and dynamic Teaching-Learning process to encourage freedom of enquiry and openness to change among the students.

* To inculcate moral, ethical values and compassionate qualities in students overall development.
